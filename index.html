After First Contact

A measured intake process before any next step is suggested.

01

Conflict and basic review

The firm first checks whether it can review the matter and identifies the immediate forum, deadline, or risk.

02

Documents requested

Relevant notices, pleadings, orders, contracts, and chronology are requested before a detailed assessment.

03

Senior lawyer evaluation

A partner or senior lawyer evaluates the material, urgency, and realistic procedural options.

04

Forum and strategy discussion

The likely court, tribunal, authority, or negotiated route is discussed with practical constraints in view.

05

Next-step clarity

The client receives a clear view of what can be done next, what information is still missing, and what timelines matter.

What to Send

Helpful documents for the first review

  • Order, case, FIR, or notice number
  • Notices, summons, or departmental communications
  • Pleadings, replies, contracts, or prior filings
  • Short timeline of relevant events
  • Next hearing, response, or limitation date
  • Documents directly connected to the dispute
